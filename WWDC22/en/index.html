About WWDC.playground

Live stream, Live coding, Tech channels and Code lab.

WWDC.playground is a free live event hosted by SwiftGG starting in 2021. And we are going to meet you soon in the second event. This year, we have invited T Salon and SwiftOldDriver together to connect with active developers in the Swift community, entrepreneurs in the Apple ecosystem, and WWDC award-winning students. They will share their own insigts about WWDC22.

In addition to the keynote review and demo live coding, we've also arranged two new phases: one is the live report from Apple Park; the other is an activity similar to Apple's Digital Lounge, which is convenient for everyone to communicate and share their learning experience and ask for help at any time.

So, we can't wait to meet you all and hope you have a happy chat and gain a lot in WWDC22.
