The WWDC.playground is coming! Organized by SwiftGG, from June 8 to 12, join the enormous Chinese developer community for a live stream with profound and experienced speakers from all over the world at no cost. We'll review and discuss the latest Apple products and technologies -- to help you keep up with the WWDC and learn how to improve your apps.
